# Dev Dz Ecommerce Store

Welcome to Dev Dz Ecommerce Store, your one-stop destination for all your shopping needs!

![Dev Dz Ecommerce Store](/Demo-Store.png)

## Features

- **Product Catalog:** Browse through our extensive catalog of products.
- **Add to Cart:** Easily add products to your cart using the Commerce.js cart API.
- **Persistent Carts:** Carts are persisted for up to 30 days, allowing visitors to resume their shopping.
- **Streamlined Checkout:** Benefit from Commerce.js's checkout tools, including country and region APIs, shipping methods API, and discounts API.
- **Customer Accounts:** Enjoy the convenience of customer logins without any server-side code.
- **Customer Order History:** Access details about your previous orders.
- **Pre-populated Checkout:** Known customer details are pre-populated in the checkout for a faster and smoother experience.
- **Payment Gateway:** Test payments using the Chec "test gateway" configured for the demo store.
- **Admin Dashboard:** Store owners can manage their store using the Commerce.js dashboard.


## About

Dev Dz Ecommerce Store is an online marketplace where you can explore a wide range of products, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout seamlessly. We strive to provide a smooth and user-friendly shopping experience.
